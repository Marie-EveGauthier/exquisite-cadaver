RULES

The game enables you to write a story collectively. Consequently, you can either start a new story or continue someone else's. The game will give you a story at random to continue, and will show you only the previous line - the beginning of the story will stay hidden.


There are few rules, but please abide by them!

  • Be mindful of spelling and grammar
  • No publicity of any kind in your stories
  • No -ism: racism, sexism, ageism, ...
  • No texting language - and no smileys either
  • A lot of creativity!
